Journalism: Magazine and Feature Writing

I'm currently a journalism senior at the University of Florida in the College of Journalism and Communications.

Originally, I'm from a little town called Green Cove Springs, which is about 30 miles southwest of Jacksonville.

I absolutely love being a reporter - It's a great opportunity to constantly meet new people, listen to their stories and learn something new every day.

When people are passionate about what they have to tell me during an interview, I don't even seen reporting as a job, but instead I look at it as a great opportunity to tell their story and share their passion.

I couldn't be more proud to be the first person in my family's generation to attend the University of Florida. I could not be more proud to be a part of the Gator Nation.
